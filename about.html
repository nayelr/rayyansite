About Me

Rayyan Rehman

I'm an engineering student at Purdue, focused on mechanical and aerospace systems, with a passion for building and creating things. I enjoy working on a variety of projects that combine engineering, design, and craftsmanship.

My interests include:

  • CNC machining and fabrication
  • Robotics and automation
  • 3D printing and rapid prototyping
  • Vintage equipment restoration
  • Custom tool and fixture design

When I'm not in the workshop or lab, I enjoy reading about new technologies, hiking, and photography.
