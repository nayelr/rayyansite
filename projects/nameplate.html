CNC'd Nameplate

December 2023 Personal Project

Overview

I designed and machined a custom nameplate using our school's CNC mill. This project allowed me to apply my CAD/CAM skills and gain hands-on experience with CNC machining.

Design Process

CAD model and toolpath simulation

The design process involved:

  • Creating the initial design in Fusion 360
  • Generating toolpaths for the CNC machine
  • Simulating the machining process to verify the design
  • Making adjustments based on material and tool constraints

Machining Process

The nameplate was machined from a solid block of aluminum using:

  • Roughing pass with a 1/4" end mill
  • Finishing pass with a 1/8" ball end mill
  • Final polishing by hand
