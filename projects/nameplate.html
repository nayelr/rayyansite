CNC'd Nameplate

April 2023 CNC Machining

Intent & Design Constraints

  • Wanted to CNC a personalized thank you sign for our robotics team mentor
  • Should be:
    • Design shaped around size of wood block
    • Aesthetically pleasing
    • Easily CNC'able

The finalized design

Methods

  • Used OnShape to create a design around the block of wood
  • Used Inventor CAM to create and simulate tool paths (see right)
  • Used team's CNC machine to mill out my design
  • Used wood finish to finish it off

A simulation of the path the machine would take

Results

A beautiful sign that commemorates a wonderful mentor!
